Anime or Not?

Merriam-Webster defines anime as: a style of animation originating in Japan that is characterized by stark colorful graphics depicting vibrant characters in action-filled plots often with fantastic or futuristic themes. While the jury's still out on whether examples influenced by Western culture like Avatar: The Last Airbender or the music video for Porter Robinson's Shelter are really anime or not, we tend to know it when we see it, as shown by the video below.

But here's the real question on everyone's mind: Can computers tell us what is and is not anime? Indeed, if this were the case, we'd have an absolute definition for what can be considered anime, and arguments like the one I mentioned above would be pointless.
